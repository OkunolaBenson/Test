Digital Forensics

Uncovering and analyzing digital evidence to protect organizations and individuals.

Forensic Analysis

Our forensic analysis service helps uncover, preserve, and interpret electronic data. We examine logs, files, systems, and devices to reconstruct digital events, identify tampering, and provide actionable evidence for investigations or legal cases.

  • Disk and file system analysis
  • Log review and event reconstruction
  • Evidence preservation and chain of custody
  • Expert reporting for litigation support

Fraud Investigation

Our fraud investigation team uses advanced forensic techniques to detect and document fraudulent digital activities. From financial crimes to insider threats, we deliver clear insights that strengthen compliance and prosecution efforts.

  • Detection of financial fraud and data manipulation
  • Phishing and insider threat investigations
  • Uncovering unauthorized system access
  • Detailed reports for regulators and legal authorities

Identity Theft Forensic

Identity theft is a growing cybercrime that threatens individuals and businesses alike. We specialize in tracking unauthorized access, fraudulent account use, and identity misuse to provide clarity and actionable recovery strategies.

  • Investigation of stolen digital identities
  • Analysis of account takeover incidents
  • Tracing fraudulent transactions
  • Recommendations for identity protection and recovery
