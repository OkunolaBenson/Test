Compliance & Training

Helping organizations meet regulatory standards while empowering employees with the knowledge to stay secure and compliant.

Regulatory Compliance

We guide businesses through complex regulatory environments, ensuring compliance with global and local cybersecurity and data protection standards. Our experts help minimize risks while avoiding costly penalties.

  • GDPR, ISO 27001, and PCI DSS compliance
  • Data protection and privacy framework design
  • Internal audits and compliance assessments
  • Third-party vendor compliance checks

Policy Development

Clear policies form the backbone of compliance. We create, update, and enforce IT and security policies aligned with industry standards and organizational goals.

  • Information security policies and procedures
  • Incident response and crisis management guidelines
  • Acceptable use and access control policies
  • Continuous review and policy lifecycle management

Cybersecurity Training

Human error is one of the biggest security risks. We provide customized training to educate employees on cybersecurity best practices and reduce organizational vulnerabilities.

  • Phishing awareness and social engineering prevention
  • Security awareness programs for staff and executives
  • Role-based training for IT and compliance officers
  • Simulated attack and response drills
