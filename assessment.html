Assessment & Security Services

Strengthening your organization’s defenses through proactive assessments and strategic controls.

Assessment, Audit & Assurance

Our Assessment, Audit & Assurance service delivers independent evaluations of your IT systems, processes, and security controls. We ensure compliance with industry standards while identifying gaps that could expose your business to risk.

  • IT risk and compliance assessments
  • Security maturity benchmarking
  • Vendor and third-party security audits
  • Assurance reports for regulators and stakeholders

Penetration Testing

Our penetration testing service simulates real-world cyberattacks to uncover vulnerabilities in your systems, applications, and networks before malicious actors exploit them.

  • Web, mobile, and API penetration testing
  • Internal & external network testing
  • Red team & adversary simulation exercises
  • Detailed remediation guidance and retesting

Ransomware Preparedness

Ransomware is one of today’s most disruptive cyber threats. We help organizations build resilience through prevention, detection, and recovery strategies tailored to their environment.

  • Ransomware readiness assessments
  • Backup and recovery validation
  • Incident response playbook development
  • Employee awareness and phishing simulations

Identity & Access Management

Strong identity and access controls are critical to protecting sensitive information. We design and implement IAM solutions that balance security with user convenience.

  • Single sign-on (SSO) implementation
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
  • Privileged access management
  • Identity governance & lifecycle management
